Read It Back. Read slowly. Notice which words land in your body — a held breath, a tightened throat, a tear, a pull in your chest. Tap or drag across those words to mark what carries weight. Not sure where to start? Look at the adjectives and descriptive phrases — they often reveal a deeper pattern.
